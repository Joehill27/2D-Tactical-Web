smroW is a 2D turn-based tactical shooter inspired by games like Worms, Shellshock Live, and Wild Ones.

How is smroW Built

smroW is built with Unity 2019.3.0a2. The game is 2D, and the source code can be viewed at https://github.com/trevinofernando/2D-Tactical-Game.

How the smroW Works

From 2 to 4 teams comprising of 2 to 4 human and AI players will be randomly spawned across a map with a chosen team. Each player will get a turn per round, rotating through each team. Player one from team one will take their turn, then player one from team two will take their turn, and so on, until everyone has taken their turns. There will be a time limit, and game mechanics that assure that games do not go on too long. To win, you must be the last team standing.

Game Environment

There will be a dynamically created tile-based map system. With each map theme, there will be different types of environmental adversaries in addition to battling with other teams.
